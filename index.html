Welcome to Mr. Barrott’s Dog Training

With over 12 years of hands-on experience in dog training and behavior, I’m passionate about creating lasting bonds between dogs and their owners through kindness and tailored training methods. Located in the USA, my approach is all about positive reinforcement, patience, and respect.

Whether you’ve welcomed a new puppy or are looking to correct behavioral issues in your dog, I’m here to guide you to a well-rounded, happy companion.

Our Comprehensive Training Services

Each training journey with your dog is unique. Explore my services which cover a wide range of needs:

  • Basic Obedience: Teaching foundational commands: sit, stay, come, heel with patient, positive techniques.
  • Advanced Training: Challenging your dog’s mind with tricks and obedience beyond basics.
  • Behavior Modification: Address concerns like barking, leash pulling, and anxiety in a gentle, effective way.
  • Puppy Socialization: Preparing your puppy for a confident life with social skills and fun exercises.
  • Private Sessions & Group Classes: Customized for your preference and lifestyle.

Essentials of Dog Care

Training is only one part of a happy dog’s life. Proper care is the foundation:

  • Balanced Nutrition: Quality food and always fresh water tailored by dog size and health.
  • Regular Exercise: Daily walks, play, and mental stimulation to keep your dog's body and mind active.
  • Routine Vet Care: Keeping vaccines current and health checks regular for lifelong wellness.
  • Consistent Grooming: Baths, brushing, nail trims, and ear care to keep your dog comfortable and clean.
  • Unconditional Love: Your time, patience, and affection are the greatest gifts to your best friend.
